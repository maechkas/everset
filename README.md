In this project I chose to work on two connected datasets from kaggle.
They collect data about **Mount Everest Climbing Deaths** since 1922  with 310  recorded deaths and   **Ascent data(1953-2020)** with 10184 recorded ascents. 
Collaborating 2 tables together provides an opportunity not only see the picture of ordinary people who climbed the mountain, but also what are the most common causes of death and how age and oxygen consumption are related to the outcome of the expedition.

This data is courtesy of The Himalayan Database- https://www.himalayandatabase.com/index.html
